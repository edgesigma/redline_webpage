THREE STEPS TO SAFER DRIVING

01

Draw your zones

Mark areas you want to avoid on the map. Freehand, box, or select an entire city boundary with one tap.

02

Get instant warnings

Every incoming ride offer is checked against your zones in real time. A clear alert tells you if pickup or dropoff falls in a no-go area.

03

Drive with confidence

Accept or decline with full awareness. No surprises, no ending up somewhere you shouldn't be. Your safety, your rules.
